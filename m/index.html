About me

My current research focuses on the syntax-semantics interfaces within the field of syntax.
My specific interest lies in investigating ellipsis phenomena and exploring diverse linguistic constructions. Through this linguistic exploration, my goal is to gain a deeper understanding of how language communicates meaning through its structures.
